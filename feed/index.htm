]]> HYGIENIC AIR HADNLING UNITS:

Hygienic Air Handling Units differ from a standard unit on lot of aspects like shaping of components, construction, cleanability, accessibility, material specification and component features.

PRODUCT FEATURES:

  • All the interior surfaces are made from Stainless-steel.
  • In order to prevent the formation and reproduction of bacteria at the junction points of the modules, the units are combined with special joining parts and a flat and smooth surface is obtained by covering them with stainless steel sheet.
  • Heater and cooler coil frames and slides are made from Stainless-steel.
  • All the filter cassettes and condensation pans are made from Stainless-steel.
  • Hygienic air handling units are used to remove bacteria and viruses that impair sterilization in environments where hygiene needs are high, such as operating rooms and laboratories. Especially hygienic air handling units provide long-term protection against the formation of micro-organisms.

]]> Heat recovery units are key to energy efficiency, offering an innovative solution in HVAC systems. These units capture and reuse thermal energy, which would otherwise be lost. They use a cross flow system to transfer heat between two air streams moving in opposite directions: one expelling exhaust air from the building and the other drawing in fresh outdoor air. This design prevents the two streams from mixing, avoiding cross-contamination.

At Klimacontra, we emphasize the importance of heat recovery units. We demonstrate how they enhance heating and cooling systems, conserve resources, and reduce costs. Our commitment aligns with the global shift towards eco-friendly practices.
