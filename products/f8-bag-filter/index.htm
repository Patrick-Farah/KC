F8 Bag Filter – KC F8

F8 Bag Filter  Made of three layer high quality synthetic media as fine and high efficiency filters after first stage filtration. Thermo-welded bags guarantee a perfect seal and durability. The filter media is constructed of a series of sealed bags for gaining high air flow at low pressure drop. Frame can be produced by galvanized metal.

FILTER CLASS: ePM1 60%

MEDIA: Synethetic

FRAME TYPE: Galvanized Steel,Plastic

APPLICATION: Prefilter for HVAC

MAX OPERATING TEMPERATURE: 75ºc
